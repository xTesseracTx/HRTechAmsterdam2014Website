    WELCOME!

    2014 is the 4th Year for HR Tech Europe, and we are delighted to welcome so many existing and new friends to what is now firmly established as the leading HR event in Europe and the fastest growing HR event in the world!

    10 Events Under One Roof in 2 Days with over 150 Speakers, and an even bigger exhibition space where we now have our own café! This year we host over 85 Exhibitors showcasing the best in HR services, solutions and applications that you can expect to find anywhere in the world. It’s unique to find so many solution and service providers at one time - make the most of it and see how they can help you unleash your people and build tomorrow’s world of work!

    Co-located within HR Tech Europe disruptHR is the evolution of 3 years of our very successful start-up competition iHR & iTalent. We will host an Innovation Zone where the start-up community including venture capitalists, consultants, advisors, and thought leaders share their insights on the future of HR; join them as they step on stage in the Zone for their exciting 15 minute show & tell sessions throughout the 2 days.

    HR Tech Europe’s 2014 agenda offers a varied timetable, an unparalleled choice of speakers, a broad range of content, and formats from Main Stage keynotes through to interactive debates and closed roundtable discussion groups. The Main Stage will host some of the most exciting thinkers and powerful keynotes in the world; Gary Hamel, Ray Wang, David McCandless, Yves Morieux, Mark Turrell.

    One-to-One Meetings are one of the most important parts of the networking at HR Tech Europe and this year it is downstairs adjacent to the Spire sponsored Amsterdam Bar. Be sure to arrange scheduled meetings here with the companies of your choice – these will enable you to maximise your networking time and take one year’s worth of meetings in the space of 2 days!

    A huge THANK YOU to all our Sponsors this year especially Oracle, Workday, SuccessFactors, Kronos, IBM, Cornerstone OnDemand and also Spire and Google for their support this year. A big thank you to you, our Delegates, for your continued support, and also to our Speakers who collectively provide a body of knowledge and experience that we believe unparalleled in the HR space today! Be sure to enjoy yourself and have fun! … and be sure to put the knowledge gained here into intelligent action when you get back to the office next week!

    Sincerely,

    Marc Coleman
