Required Credentials for a Media Pass
To qualify for a complimentary media pass the applicant must cover the industry on a regular basis by writing frequent reports or publishing relevant articles on the industry that are not solicited by an exhibiting company and are not confidential. Private Consultantss who are paid by an individual company are not eligible for a media pass, and should request a pass from the sponsoring company.
